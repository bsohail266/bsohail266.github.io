Privacy Policy for Body In White

Effective Date: March 2, 2026

Welcome to Body In White (BIW). We provide automotive news, technical blogs, and digital assets. This policy explains how we handle your data in compliance with India’s DPDPA 2023 and IT Rules 2026.

1. Data We Collect (The "What")

We collect personal data only when you take a "clear affirmative action" (like clicking 'Subscribe' or 'Buy'):

  • Identity & Contact: Name, email address, and professional affiliation (for newsletters).
  • Commercial & Transactional: Billing address, purchase history, and GST details (if applicable) for the sale of BIW designs or content.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information to ensure 3D BIW models and technical diagrams render correctly.
  • Cookies: Identifiers used for site analytics, "Shopping Cart" memory, and user preferences (e.g., dark mode).

2. Purpose of Processing (The "Why")

In line with the Purpose Limitation principle, your data is used only to:

  • Deliver BIW news, updates, and engineering blog posts.
  • Process payments and deliver digital products (designs/CAD files).
  • Respond to technical queries or support requests.
  • Prevent fraud and ensure website security.

3. Consent & Withdrawal

Your use of this site is based on free, specific, and informed consent.

  • You may withdraw your consent at any time (e.g., by clicking "Unsubscribe").
  • Withdrawing consent for marketing does not affect your access to previously purchased digital products.

4. Storage, Security & Third-Party Sharing

  • Data Minimization: We only collect what is strictly necessary.
  • Storage: Data is stored on secure, encrypted servers. We retain transaction records for the duration required by Indian Tax Law (typically 7 years).
  • Security: We implement "Reasonable Security Practices" (encryption, firewalls) to prevent data breaches.
  • Third-Parties: We share data with PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways (like Razorpay) and cloud hosts. We never sell your engineering data to third-party marketing firms.

5. AI Disclosure & Metadata (2026 Mandate)

  • Synthetic Content: Any AI-generated BIW visualizations or technical summaries are clearly labeled as "AI-Generated."
  • Traceability: We embed permanent metadata in AI-generated digital assets as per the 2026 IT Rules to ensure transparency and provenance.

6. Children’s Privacy

  • Verifiable Consent: bodyinwhite.in is intended for engineering students and professionals (18+).
  • We do not knowingly process data of children under 18. If we discover such data has been collected without verifiable parental consent, it will be deleted within 48 hours.

7. Your Rights (Data Principal Rights)

Under Indian law, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a summary of your personal data and processing activities.
  • Right to Correction/Erasure: Update inaccurate data or request deletion (Right to be Forgotten).
  • Right to Nominate: Appoint an individual to manage your data rights in the event of death or incapacity.
  • Right to Redressal: Seek assistance for any privacy-related grievances.

8. External Links

Our BIW news updates often link to external OEM websites or engineering journals. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external entities; please review their policies when you leave our site.
